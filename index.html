Ternary scale properties

This microtonal theory calculator is about ternary scales, scales with three step sizes. It specializes in aberrismic theory, developed by groundfault, inthar, and others.

Made by inthar. The source code is available on GitHub.
     

Enter a specific ternary scale word, without spaces, using L, m, and s (use lowercase m for medium):

Enter the numbers of step sizes as steps get smaller, as positive numbers separated by spaces. For example, "3 2 2" means 3L2m2s.
